No 2 The Process

The plan, in the process of making a site system starts with the planning process. At this stage you determine the purpose of the site to be created. You also analyze and collect data needed by the site, then place some of these needs into the site that will be developed. To work more professionally, you must create a work schedule.

Design, from information and data collected at the planning stage, the next stage you design the front page and page views on the site. Design is needed to give your site a beauty. This can be a combination of unique colors, layout, fonts that make the contents of your site very attractive and easy to read. Your site design must also provide an unforgettable image that will distinguish your site from other rivals. Decisions about how your site should interact with visitors make it easier for them to navigate on your site and how information is categorized so that visitors can access the information they want as quickly as possible is determined at this stage.
